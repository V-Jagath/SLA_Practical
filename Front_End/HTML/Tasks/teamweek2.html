Conventional Teaching Method

Conventional Teaching Method (also known as Traditional Teaching Method) refers to the old or standard way of teaching that has been used in classrooms for many decades. Here's a simple breakdown

Definition:

Conventional teaching method is a teacher-centered approach where the teacher is the main source of knowledge and students are passive learners.
