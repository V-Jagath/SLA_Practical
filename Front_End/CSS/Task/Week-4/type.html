Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our typing courses

What is the minimum typing speed required for government jobs?

For most government typing jobs in India, the minimum required speed is 35-40 words per minute (WPM) in English and 30-35 WPM in regional languages like Tamil. However, higher speeds give you a competitive edge in exams and job applications.

How long does it take to learn typing?

With regular practice, most students can learn basic typing in 2-3 weeks and become proficient within 2-3 months. Our courses are structured to help you achieve government job requirements within 3-4 months of training.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we have tie-ups with several placement agencies and companies that regularly hire our students. We also provide guidance for government typing tests and help with exam preparation.

What are the batch timings?

We offer flexible batch timings throughout the day: Morning (6 AM - 11 AM), Afternoon (12 PM - 4 PM), and Evening (5 PM - 9 PM). Weekend batches are also available for working professionals.

Is the certificate recognized for government jobs?

Yes, our certificate is recognized by various government departments and private organizations. We also prepare students for specific government typing tests that require certification.
