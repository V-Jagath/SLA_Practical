IPL Ticket Booking

To book IPL tickets for matches in Chennai, you can visit the official IPL website and District by Zomato.You can also check BookMyShow for upcoming matches. Ticket prices vary by stand and are expected to be between Rs 400 and Rs 30,000, according to The Indian Express.

Tips for booking:

Check prices and availability: Prices vary depending on the stand and the teams playing.


Book early:

Popular matches tend to sell out quickly, so it's best to book your tickets as soon as possible. Consider online and offline options: In addition to online platforms, some franchises also sell tickets at the venue. Be aware of ticket limits: Some matches may have a limit on the number of tickets you can purchase per transaction.
